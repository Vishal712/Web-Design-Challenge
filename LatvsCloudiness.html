Cloudiness


Comparing Lattitude to cloudiness in each city.

This plot shows a comparison of Lattitude and the cloudiness recorded in each city. Just like the Humidity plot, there isn't a clear cut visual cue for a specific pattern that can be found here. The only trend based solely on the plot is that cloudiness seems to be on the extremes rather than clustered in the middle. This means that there a lot of cities clustered at around 100% cloudiness, and then clustered also at 0% cloudiness.
With more information of how the cloudiness was recorded, as well as the fact that there seems to be a longer cluster of 100% cloudiness of lattitudes over 0, we can potentially say that the farther away from the equator there are more cities with either 0 or 100% cloudiness.
