Summary: Lattitude vs X

The purpose of this project was to see a comparison of multiple observations and the lattitude of cities recorded. In general, this can tell us what happens when we go closer or farther from the equator.
This was accomplished using an OpenWeatherAPI, used to get information from over 500 cities, returning a plethora of information as it relates to weather. Then after getting the information from the API, we stored it in a Pandas Dataframe, and started to plot certain aspects. This includes the Latitude of the city and the Temperature, the cloudiness, humidity, and wind speed.
This box contains an example of one of the four plots included in the site, with the rest shown to the right, and also on the Comparison page and Plots dropdown on the top navbar. Each plot also includes a description of the trend and observations found.
