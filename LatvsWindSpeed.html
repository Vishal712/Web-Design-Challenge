Wind Speed


Comparing Lattitude to wind speed in each city.

This plot shows a comparison of Lattitude and the wind speed recorded in each city. Overall, the distribution doesn't follow a bell curve or a straightforwards pattern that maybe the Max Temperature plot may exhibit, but as the lattitude enters around 40 to 70, we can see that there is a large cluster of wind speed from about 3mph to around 12mph.
When it comes to larger windspeeds, there doesn't seem to be a clear trend, as there aren't a lot of cities with wind speeds reaching above 20mph. However, there are more cities with a faster wind speed with lattitudes above 0 than those in the negatives.
